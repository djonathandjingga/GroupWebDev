Pancakes
Ingredients (Serving: 2-3 people):

- 100g plain flour

- 2 large eggs

- 300ml milk

- 1 tbsp sunflower or vegetable oil (a little extra for
   frying)

- lemon wedges (to serve), optional

- caster sugar (to serve), optional
Instructions:

1. Put 100g plain flour, 2 large eggs, 300ml milk, 1
    tbsp sunflower or vegetable oil and a pinch of salt
    into a bowl then whisk to a smooth batter.

2. Set aside for 30 mins to rest if you have time, or
    start cooking straight away.

3. Set a medium frying pan over a medium heat and
    carefully wipe it with some oiled kitchen paper.

4. When hot, cook your pancakes for 1 min on each
    side until golden, keeping them warm in a low
    oven as you go.

5. Serve with lemon wedges and caster sugar, or your
    favorite filling.
