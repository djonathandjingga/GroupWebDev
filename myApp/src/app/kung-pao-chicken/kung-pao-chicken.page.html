Kung Pao Chicken
Ingredients (Serving: 2-3 people):

- 1 lbs boneless skinless chicken breast halves

- 2 tbsp white wine

- 2 tbsp soy sauce

- 2 tbsp sesame oil (divided)

- 2 tbsp dissolved cornstarch

- 1 oz hot chile paste

- 1 tsp distilled white vinegar

- 2 tsp brown sugar

- 4 green onions (chopped)

- 1 tbsp chopped garlic

- 8 oz water chesnuts

- 4 oz chopped peanuts
Instructions:

1. To make marinade: Combine 1 tbsp wine, tbsp soy
    sauce, 1 tbsp oil and tbsp cornstarch mixture and
    mix together. Place chicken pieces in a glass dish
    or bowl and add marinade. Toss to coat. Cover
    dish and place in refrigerator for about 30 minutes.

2. To make sauce: In a small bowl, combine 1 tbsp
    wine, 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp oil, 1 tbsp corstarch
    mixture, chili paste, vinegar and sugar. Mix
    together and add green onion, garlic, water
    chesnuts and peanuts. In a medium skillet, heat
    sauce slowly until aromatic.

3. Meanwhile, remove chicken from marinade and
    saute in a large skillet until meat is white and juices
    run clear. When sauce is aromatic, add sauteed
    chicken to it and let simmer together until sauce
    thickens.
