Cosmopolitan
Ingredients (Serving: 1 people):

- 45ml lemon vodka

- 15ml triple sec

- 30ml cranberry juice

- 10ml lime juice
Instructions:

1. Shake ingredients in a cocktail shaker with ice
    and strain into a cocktail glass.

2. To make the garnish: hold a 3cm round piece of
    orange zest about 10cm above your cosmo and
    very carefully wave it over a lit match or lighter
    flame. Bend the outer edge of the zest in towards
    the flame so that the orange oils are released,
    then drop the zest into your drink.
