Caipirinha
Ingredients (Serving: 1 people):

- 1 lime (cut into wedges)

- 2 tsp sugar

- 2 oz cachaça

- lime wheel (garnish)
Instructions:

1. In a double Old Fashioned glass, muddle the sugar
    and lime.

2. Fill with ice, add the cachaça, and stir briefly.

3. Garnish with a lime wheel.
