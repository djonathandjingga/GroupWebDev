Jamaican Jerk Chicken
Ingredients (Serving: 6-8 people):

- 1 medium onion (coarsely chopped)

- 3 medium scallions (chopped)

- 2 Scotch bonnet chiles (chopped)

- 2 garlic cloves (chopped)

- 1 tbsp five-spice powder

- 1 tbsp allspice berries (coarsely ground)

- 1 tbsp coarsely ground peppert

- 1 tsp dried thyme (crumbled)

- 1 tsp freshly grated nutmeg

- 1 tsp salt

- 0.5 cup soy sauce

- 1 tbsp vegetable oil

- two 3.5- to 4-lbs chickens (quartered)
Instructions:

1. In a food processor, combine the onion, scallions,
    chiles, garlic, five-spice powder, allspice,
    pepper, thyme, nutmeg and salt; process to a
    coarse paste. With the machine on, add the soy
    sauce and oil in a steady stream. Pour the
    marinade into a large, shallow dish, add the
    chicken and turn to coat. Cover and refrigerate
    overnight. Bring the chicken to room temperature
    before proceeding.

2. Light a grill. Grill the chicken over a medium-hot
    fire, turning occasionally, until well browned and
    cooked through, 35 to 40 minutes. (Cover the grill
    for a smokier flavor). Transfer the chicken to a
    platter and serve.
