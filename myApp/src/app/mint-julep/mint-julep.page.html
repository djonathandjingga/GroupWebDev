Mint Julep
Ingredients (Serving: 1 people):

- 0.25 oz simple syrup

- 8 mint leaves

- 2 oz bourbon

- bitters (garnish)

- mint sprig (garnish)
Instructions:

1. In a Julep cup or rocks glass, lightly muddle the mint
    and syrup.

2. Add the bourbon and pack tightly with crushed ice.

3. Stir until the cup is frosted on the outside.

4. Top with more crushed ice to form an ice dome and
    garnish with a mint sprig and a few optional drops of
    bitters.
