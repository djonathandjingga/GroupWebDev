Calzone
Ingredients (Serving: 2 people):

For the dough:

- 250g plain flour

- 2 tsp baking powder

- large pinch salt

- 2 large free-range eggs (beaten)

- 2 tbsp sunflower oil

For the filling:

- 50g mozzarella (grated or torn)

- 400g tin chopped tomatoes (cooked in saucepan
  until reduced in half)

- 4 stalks sprouting broccoli (chopped)

- 0.5 onion (sliced)

- 50g cheddar (grated)

- 2 rashers bacon (cooked and chopped)

- 1 garlic clove (finely chopped)

- 2 tbsp fresh herbs, or 1 tsp dried herbs of your
  choice (such as basil, oregano, or parsley)
Instructions:

1. For the dough, put all the ingredients in a large
    bowl and mix together, add 4-6 tbsp water,
    one tbsp at a time, until the dough comes together.

2. On a floured work surface roll out the dough into
    two circles, roughly the same size.

3. Preheat the oven to 220C/200C Fan/Gas 6.
    Place a baking tray or pizza stone in the oven to
    heat up.

4. Mix all the fillings ingredients together and
    divide in half. Each half on one side of each
    circle of dough and fold the dough over to create a
    pastry shape. Seal the edge using your fingers or
    a fork.

5. Place the calzones on baking paper and carefully
    transfer to the preheated baking tray or pizza stone.
    Cook for 15-20 minutes, or until the dough is crisp
    and browned.
