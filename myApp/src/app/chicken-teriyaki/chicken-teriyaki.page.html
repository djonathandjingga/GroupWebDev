Chicken Teriyaki
Ingredients (Serving: 3-4 people):

- 1.25 lbs boneless skinless chicken breast (diced)

- 1 tbsp olive oil

- 0.25 cup low-sodium soy sauce

- 0.25 cup water

- 2 tbsp honey

- 1.5 tbsp packed light brown sugar

- 1 tbsp rice vinegar

- 0.25 tsp toasted sesame oil

- 2 tsp peeled and minced fresh ginger

- 2 tsp peeled and minced fresh garlic (2 cloves)

- 2 tsp cornstarch
Instructions:

1. Heat olive oil in a 12-inch non-stick skillet
    over medium high heat.

2. Add in chicken and let brown on buttom about 3-4
    minutes then flip and continue to cook until center
    registers about 160 degress.

3. While chicken is cooking, in a small mixing bowl
    whisk together soy sauce, water, honey, brown
    sugar, rice vinegar, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, and
    cornstarch.

4. Stir the sauce in with the cooked chicken and
    continue to cook and toss until sauce has
    thickened.

5. Serve warm garnished with green onions.
