Split Pea Soup
Ingredients (Serving: 5-6 people):

- 1 cup chopped yellow onions

- 2 cloves garlic (minced)

- 0.125 cup good olive oil

- 0.5 tsp dried oregano

- 1.5 tsp kosher salt

- 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper

- 2 cups medium-diced carrots (3 to 4 carrots)

- 1 cup medium-diced red boiling unpeeled potatoes
  (3 small)

- 1 lbs dried split green peas

- 8 cups chicken stock or water
Instructions:

1. In a 4-quart stockpot on medium heat, saute the
    onions and garlic with the olive oil, oregano, salt,
    and pepper until the onions are translucent.

2. Add carrots, potatoes, 0.5 lbs of split peas,
    and chicken stock.

3. Bring to boil, then simmer uncovered for 40
    minutes.

4. Skim off the foam while cooking.

5. Add the remaining split peas and continue to
    simmer for another 40 minutes.

6. Stir frequently to keep the solids from burning on
    the bottom.

7. Taste for salt and pepper.

8. Serve hot.
