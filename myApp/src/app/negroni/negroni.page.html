Negroni
Ingredients (Serving: 1 people):

- 1 oz gin

- 1 oz Campari

- 1 oz sweet vermouth

- orange peel (garnish)
Instructions:

1. Add all the ingredients into a mixing glass with ice,
    and stir until well-chilled.

2. Strain into a rocks glass filled with large ice cubes.

3. Garnish with an orange peel.
