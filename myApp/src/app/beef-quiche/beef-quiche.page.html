Beef Quiche
Ingredients (Serving: 6 people):

- 250g minced beef

- 1 onion (mediumly diced)

- 1 tbsp oil

- 0.5 tsp salt

- 0.5 tsp dried oregano

- 0.5 tsp garlic powder

- 0.25 tsp ground black pepper

- 3 eggs

- 0.5 cup milk

- 0.5 cup mayonnaise

- 1 cup cheddar cheese (grated)

- 1 cup Swiss cheese (grated

- 1 sheet uncooked puff pastry
Instructions:

1. Heat the oil in a frypan. Cook the minced beef and
    onion until the beef is browned and the onion is
    soft. Remove from the heat.

2. Stir in the salt, oregano, garlic powder and pepper.

3. In a bowl, beat the eggs, milk and mayonnaise
    together, then stir into the meat mixture. Fold in the
    cheeses.

4. Line a lightly greased 23cm pie plate with the
    sheet of puff pastry. Pour in the mince mixture.

5. Bake uncovered at 190 degrees C for 40-45
    minutes, or until it is golden and puffed in the
    centre.

6. Rest for 5 minutes before cutting.
